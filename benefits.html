Benefits

There are many benefits of participating in Colors of Humanity Art Gallery's juried art shows. -Increase Exposure -International Visibility -Add more traffic to your website where buyers can learn more about you and see more of your work. (All accepted pieces will have a link back to the artist website) -Win Prize Money -Boost your resume -Artist keeps 100% of the sale. Colors of Humanity Art Gallery does not collect any commission. Sale is made solely between the artist and the buyer. -Gain low cost experience -Your work will be archived and available to see over and over -No fear of art work being damaged in a physical show -No cost of transporting art work -Your art can be in another show at the same time it is in this one. -Helping worthy organizations
